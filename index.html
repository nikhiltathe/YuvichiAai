About Me

Your trusted guide through motherhood

Hi, I'm Yuvichi — A Mother, A Healer, A Guide

With over 10 years of experience in prenatal and postnatal wellness, I've dedicated my life to supporting women through the most transformative journey of their lives.

My holistic approach combines gentle yoga, breathing techniques, nutrition guidance, and emotional wellness to prepare your body and mind for the beautiful experience of motherhood.

Every woman's journey is unique, and I'm here to walk alongside you — offering strength, knowledge, and the warmth of a community that truly understands.
